Before you download ANYTHING from a friend, make sure it's a trusted source.

Tips:

  • Look at the address bar. Make sure it's a trusted domain (i.e. steampowered.com or steamcommunity.com).
  • If you don't know the domain in the address bar, Google it!
  • Check file extensions. Some file extensions scammers have been taking advantage of are .scr and .exe
  • Ask me for help. I know what I'm doing. Friend me on Steam and I'll tell you if a link is bad or not.
  • Steam will never ask you to download a file. Unless you're installing Steam or downloading a game, Steam won't ask you to download files.
